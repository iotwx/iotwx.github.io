Station Designs and Inspiration

3D-Printed Stations

  • 3D-PAWS original project and design by Martin Steinson and Paul Kucera UCAR/COMET. The station is non-commercial, Raspberry-pi based and also >80% 3D-printed.
  • LTB Weather station by Rob W. Lakes on Thingiverse.

Raspberry Pi-based Weather Stations

  • SkyWeather is a low-cost, commercial Raspberry Pi weather station kit for the cloud based on the [SwitchDoc PiWeather board]() and Blynk.io. Much of is NOT 3D printed, but there are a few 3D printed parts.
  • RasperryWeather.com is a nice resource for Pi-based weather stations.
